## Elden Ring — Shadow of the Erdtree DLC Bosses

### General Tips
Shadow of the Erdtree bosses are significantly harder than base-game bosses. Collect Scadutree Fragments (found throughout the Shadow Realm) to raise your Scadutree Blessing level — this is the primary way to increase your damage and damage absorption against DLC enemies. Aim for at least blessing level 15–20 before attempting late-game bosses.

## Boss Strategies

### Divine Beast Dancing Lion (Belurat)
**Runes:** 120,000 | **ID:** 1
The first major boss of the DLC. Fights in two phases: the first phase focuses on lightning and wind attacks; the second phase adds snowstorm and frost. 
- Stay beneath it during lightning attacks to avoid the vertical strikes.
- The spinning wind attack can be dodged by rolling into the rotation.
- Stockpile Preserving Boluses to counter frostbite buildup in phase two.
- Recommended Scadutree Blessing: 2–5.

### Rellana, Twin Moon Knight
**Runes:** 240,000 | **ID:** 2
A fast, aggressive duelist who wields both flame and moonlight swords. One of the earlier "wall" bosses that punishes passive play.
- She chains combos aggressively — learn which attacks have openings after the last hit.
- Her moonlight wave projectiles travel slowly; sidestep rather than roll through them.
- High fire and magic resistance helps. Lightning is her weakness.
- Recommended Scadutree Blessing: 5–8.

### Messmer the Impaler
**Runes:** 400,000 | **ID:** 3
A mid-to-late DLC boss and a story centrepiece. Wields a spear and fire snake magic.
- Phase one: punish the end of his spear combos with counter-attacks. His grab attack is telegraphed by him extending his arm — roll away immediately.
- Phase two: he gains flame snake extensions on his attacks. Roll through the flame, not away from it.
- High fire resistance (Flamedrake Talisman, Fire Resist armour) dramatically reduces his damage.
- Recommended Scadutree Blessing: 12–15.

### Metyr, Mother of Fingers
**Runes:** 420,000 | **ID:** 4
A late-game cosmic horror boss. Deals gravity and holy damage with wide sweeping attacks.
- Her beam attacks have clear wind-up animations — bait them and punish after.
- Stay close during most of her attacks; her ranged options are more dangerous than her melee.
- Holy damage negation (Haligdrake Talisman) is very effective here.
- Recommended Scadutree Blessing: 15–18.

### Putrescent Knight
**Runes:** 220,000 | **ID:** 5
A spectral knight atop a decayed horse. Deals fire and sleep damage; a major sleep proc can end the fight early if you're unprepared.
- Keep Clarifying Boluses on hand to cure sleep buildup.
- The horse charges are the most dangerous attack — dodge to the side, not backward.
- After the charge, the knight is briefly vulnerable — this is your main damage window.
- Recommended Scadutree Blessing: 8–12.

## General Combat Advice
- **Scadutree Fragments** are the single most important upgrade — prioritise finding them over rushing bosses.
- **Positioning:** Most DLC bosses punish passive backpedalling. Play aggressively and stay mid-range to bait attacks.
- **Summons:** Spirit Ash summons (especially Mimic Tear or Black Knife Tiche) remain effective in the DLC. Don't hesitate to use them.
- **Rune farming:** The boss rune rewards are substantial — if stuck, clearing easier areas and levelling up is always a valid strategy.
